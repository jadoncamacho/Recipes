Salmon Bake

Description

Enjoy a simple yet delicious dinner with this salmon bake! Perfect for fans of simple rice plates while being quick, easy, and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 large salmon filet
  • 1-2 packs cherry tomatoes
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 8-10 cloves of garlic
  • 1 Tbsp dry thyme
  • 1 Tbsp dry marjoram
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. step 1
  2. step2

heat olive oil sautee onions and garlic (until onions are half cooked) throw cherry tomatoes in sautee for another ~1 minute add dry seasonings preheat oven to 350F add small amount of juice to pyrax baking containter put salmon in put rest of sautee contents in cover with foil and bake until bubbles form (~30-40 minutes)
